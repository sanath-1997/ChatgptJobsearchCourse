This course explains a clear and structured way to use ChatGPT as a helpful assistant in your job search. Instead of manually searching for jobs, applying, and customizing each application, this system uses AI to make the process faster and easier.

Identifying Job Titles and ATS Keywords

Mastering the Google XYZ Formula

Learn how to convert plain resume points into achievement-focused statements.

5.1 What Is the Google XYZ Formula?

The Google XYZ formula is a simple way to write strong resume points.

The format is:

Accomplished X
Measured by Y
By doing Z

5.2 Example

Instead of writing:

“Handled sales calls”

You can write:

“Increased sales conversions by 20% in 3 months by improving follow-up calls and customer tracking.”

5.3 Why This Formula Is Effective

This method turns simple statements into clear achievements. It helps hiring managers quickly understand:

✓What you achieved
✓How it was measured
✓What actions you took

This makes your resume more effective.
